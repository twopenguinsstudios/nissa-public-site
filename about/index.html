Our story

We built the tool we wished we had.

Before Nissa, we spent years in procurement and finance operations at mid-market companies. We saw the same pattern everywhere: contracts get signed, then disappear. They live in email inboxes, shared drives, and the memory of the person who signed them.

Renewals get missed. Pricing never gets benchmarked. Nobody knows what's in the contracts until something goes wrong. We tried the big CLM platforms — they were built for Fortune 500 legal teams, not a VP of Finance at a 300-person company trying to keep track of 200 vendor contracts.

When large language models became capable enough to reliably extract contract terms, we knew the moment had arrived. The technology could finally meet the problem where it lives — in the inbox, in Slack, in the tools teams already use every day.

"Most CLMs store contracts. We built Nissa to run them — to turn static documents into operational intelligence that works where your team already works."

That's Nissa. Forward a contract. Get answers. No portal. No migration. No adoption problem.

What we believe

Principles we build on.

Zero-friction or bust

If it requires training, migration, or a new portal, most teams won't use it. We obsess over making Nissa work where people already work.

Honest AI

AI should show its work. We surface confidence levels, link to source text, and never present uncertain extractions as facts. Your team stays in control.

Your data is yours

We never train on your contracts. We never share them. We encrypt everything. When you leave, we delete everything. No exceptions.
