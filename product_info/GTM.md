# Nissa — Go-To-Market Strategy

## Positioning

### One-liner
"Most CLMs store contracts. Nissa runs them."

### Elevator Pitch
Nissa is an AI-powered contract intelligence platform for procurement and finance teams. Forward a contract via email, get instant analysis: pricing benchmarks, risk alerts, renewal tracking. No portals. No setup. No adoption problem. And unlike every CLM on the market, Nissa doesn't stop at the signature. That's where it starts.

### Category
AI-first lightweight CLM / Post-signature contract intelligence

### Key Differentiator
Every competitor optimizes for pre-signature workflow. Nissa optimizes for post-signature intelligence. 90% of contract value lives after the signature. That's where money leaks, renewals get missed, and obligations go untracked. Nissa is the only tool built specifically for that reality.

## Competitive Positioning

## Sales Motion

### Phase 1: Founder-Led Sales (Now)
- Pat and team run all demos personally
- Target: 3-5 design partners in 90 days
- Channel: warm outreach, LinkedIn, targeted cold email
- Goal: validate product-market fit, collect case studies

### Phase 2: Repeatable Sales (Post-5 customers)
- Inbound SDR agent handles qualification and booking
- Content engine drives organic inbound
- Demo prep agent personalizes every call
- Goal: predictable pipeline

### Sales Cycle
- **Expected:** 2-4 weeks for Starter, 4-8 weeks for Pro/Scale
- **Champion:** VP Finance or Head of Procurement
- **Blocker:** Legal (manage by positioning as post-signature, not pre-signature)
- **Accelerator:** Show them their own renewal risk in the demo

### Demo Flow
1. Ask about their current contract situation (where do contracts live?)
2. Show the email-forward flow (60 seconds to first analysis)
3. Show a renewal calendar with fake auto-renewal risk
4. Show pricing benchmark across vendors
5. Ask: "How many auto-renewals hit you last year?"
6. Close: "Want to try it with 10 of your real contracts this week?"
