# Nissa — Project Overview

> Most CLMs store contracts. Nissa runs them.

## What Nissa Is

Nissa is an AI-first, lightweight contract intelligence platform built for procurement and finance teams at mid-market companies. It's not another CLM. It's the layer that makes contracts usable after they're signed.

Traditional CLMs focus on storage and workflow before signature. Nissa focuses on what happens after: tracking renewals, enforcing procurement policy, benchmarking pricing, and surfacing contract terms in real time inside the tools teams already use (email, Slack, Microsoft Teams).

**The core insight:** 90% of contract value lives post-signature. That's where contracts fail, where money leaks, and where nobody is watching. Nissa watches.

## How It Works

1. **Forward a contract** to nissa@yourdomain.com. No portal. No login. Just hit send.
2. **Nissa reads and analyzes** — extracts pricing, terms, clauses, and compares against historical contracts.
3. **Get a smart reply** — pricing benchmarks, risk alerts (auto-renewals, missing SLAs), and a suggested action.

No dashboards. No migration projects. Contracts become usable the moment they arrive.

## Why "AI-First Lightweight CLM"

Most CLMs are built around storage and workflow automation. They assume you'll migrate all your contracts, train your team on a new system, and enforce adoption. That's why 47% of finance leaders say integration is the biggest CLM barrier, and why most CLMs end up as expensive filing cabinets.

Nissa takes the opposite approach:
- **No migration required.** Contracts come in naturally via email, Slack, Teams, shared drives
- **No new workflows.** Nissa runs inside the tools your team already uses
- **No adoption problem.** If your team can forward an email, they can use Nissa
- **Value on day one.** You don't need perfect data to start getting answers
- **Live in under a week.** Not months. Not quarters. Days.

## What Happens After the Signature

This is Nissa's wedge. Every other tool optimizes for getting to a signature. Nissa optimizes for everything after:

- **Renewal tracking:** Never get auto-renewed into a bad contract again
- **Pricing intelligence:** Benchmark vendor pricing across your portfolio. Know if you're overpaying before you negotiate.
- **Obligation monitoring:** SLAs, termination windows, notice periods. All tracked automatically.
- **Policy enforcement:** Procurement rules applied as contracts arrive. Compliant contracts auto-approved, exceptions flagged.
- **Savings agents:** Always-on monitoring for renegotiation opportunities and cost reduction.
