How it works

From any inbox to every answer your team needs.

STEP 01

Send contracts in any way

Forward by email, drop in Slack or Teams, sync a Drive or OneDrive folder. Nissa picks them up automatically.

STEP 02

Policy check on arrival

Define your policies once — cyber insurance limits, indemnity caps, DPA requirements, data residency, pricing escalators. Nissa flags every contract that exceeds them.

STEP 03

Redline proposals

Nissa drafts redlines for the standard stuff. Your legal team takes it from there.
